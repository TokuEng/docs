## Setting Up Integrations

To connect an integration:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations** in TGA
2. Select the integration you want to configure
3. Follow the setup wizard to connect your account
4. Test the connection before going live

For help setting up integrations, contact your Toku account manager or [reach out to support](/support/contact).
