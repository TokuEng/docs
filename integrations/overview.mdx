## How Integrations Work

### Custody Provider Flow

1. **Connect** — Link your custody account in Toku
2. **Authorize** — Approve Toku's access permissions
3. **Configure** — Set up distribution workflows
4. **Operate** — Toku initiates transfers, you approve in your custody platform

### HRIS Sync Flow

1. **Connect** — Authorize access to your HRIS
2. **Map fields** — Configure how data maps to Toku
3. **Sync** — Employee data updates automatically
4. **Review** — Verify synced data in Toku
