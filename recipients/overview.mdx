## What you'll see

### Grant Summary
View all your active grants, including:
- Total tokens granted
- Tokens vested
- Tokens claimed
- Upcoming vesting events

### Vesting Schedule
See when your tokens vest with a visual timeline showing past and future vesting dates.

### Claim Status
Track the status of your token claims from request to delivery.

---

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept your invitation">
    Click the link in your invitation email to access TGA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your grants">
    See what tokens you've been granted and your vesting schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a wallet">
    Add a wallet address where you want to receive tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim tokens">
    Once tokens vest, claim them to your connected wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>
