## Overview

Once tokens vest according to your grant's schedule, you can claim them to your wallet. This guide walks you through the claiming process.

## Prerequisites

Before claiming tokens, ensure you have:

- A connected wallet address in your TGA profile
- Vested tokens available to claim
- Small amount of native tokens for gas fees (if applicable)

## Setting Up Your Wallet

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Settings">
  Click your profile icon and select **Settings**.
</Step>

<Step title="Add Wallet Address">
  In the **Wallet** section, enter your wallet address where you want to receive tokens.

  <Warning>
  Double-check your wallet address. Token transfers cannot be reversed if sent to the wrong address.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify Ownership">
  You may need to verify wallet ownership by signing a message. Follow the on-screen prompts.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Claiming Vested Tokens

<Steps>
<Step title="View Available Tokens">
  From your dashboard, view tokens available to claim under **Vested Balance**.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Claim">
  Click the **Claim Tokens** button.
</Step>

<Step title="Review Details">
  Review the claim details:
  - Token amount
  - Destination wallet
  - Any applicable fees
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm Claim">
  Click **Confirm** to initiate the transfer.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
  The transfer will be processed. You'll receive confirmation once complete.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Claim Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Processing** | Claim submitted, transfer in progress |
| **Completed** | Tokens successfully sent to your wallet |
| **Failed** | Transfer failed - contact support |

## Viewing Claim History

Track all your past claims:

1. Navigate to **History** in the sidebar
2. View all past claims with:
   - Claim date
   - Token amount
   - Transaction hash
   - Status

<Tip>
Click the transaction hash to view the transfer on the blockchain explorer.
</Tip>

## Automatic Claims

Some organizations enable automatic claiming. When enabled:

- Vested tokens are automatically sent to your wallet
- No manual action required
- You'll receive notification when transfers complete

Check with your administrator if automatic claims are available.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My claim is stuck on Processing">
    Claims typically complete within a few minutes. If longer:
    - Check network status for congestion
    - Contact support if pending over 24 hours
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I entered the wrong wallet address">
    Contact your administrator immediately. If tokens haven't been sent yet, they may be able to update the address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I don't see my tokens in my wallet">
    - Verify the transaction completed in TGA
    - Check you're viewing the correct token in your wallet
    - You may need to add the token contract to your wallet
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my wallet address?">
    Yes, go to **Settings** > **Wallet** to update your address. Changes apply to future claims only.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tax Considerations

<Note>
Token grants may have tax implications. Consult with a tax professional regarding reporting requirements in your jurisdiction.
</Note>

TGA provides transaction records for your tax reporting:

1. Go to **Reports** > **Tax Documents**
2. Select the tax year
3. Download your transaction summary
