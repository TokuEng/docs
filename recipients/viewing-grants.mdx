## Overview

As a token grant recipient, you can view all your grants, track vesting progress, and see upcoming distributions in the TGA portal.

## Accessing Your Grants

<Steps>
<Step title="Log In to TGA">
  Go to [tga.app.toku.com](https://tga.app.toku.com) and log in with your credentials.
</Step>

<Step title="View Dashboard">
  Your dashboard shows a summary of all your grants:
  - Total tokens granted
  - Tokens vested (available)
  - Tokens unvested (pending)
  - Upcoming vesting events
</Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Grant Details

Click on any grant to view its details:

### Grant Summary

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Grant Date** | When the grant was issued |
| **Total Amount** | Total tokens in this grant |
| **Token Type** | The type of token granted |
| **Vesting Schedule** | How tokens vest over time |
| **Status** | Current grant status |

### Vesting Progress

The vesting progress section shows:

- **Vested**: Tokens that have vested and are available
- **Unvested**: Tokens still subject to vesting
- **Next Vesting Date**: When more tokens will vest
- **Next Vesting Amount**: How many tokens will vest next

## Vesting Timeline

The timeline view displays:

- Past vesting events (completed)
- Upcoming vesting events (scheduled)
- Cliff dates (if applicable)
- Grant completion date

<Tip>
Enable email notifications to receive alerts before vesting events.
</Tip>

## Grant Statuses Explained

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Active** | Grant is active and vesting normally |
| **Paused** | Vesting is temporarily paused |
| **Completed** | All tokens have vested |
| **Pending** | Grant created but not yet started |

## Notification Settings

Stay informed about your grants:

1. Click your profile icon
2. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
3. Configure alerts for:
   - Upcoming vesting events
   - Tokens ready to claim
   - Grant status changes

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do my tokens vest?">
    Check your grant details for the vesting schedule. The timeline shows all upcoming vesting dates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my grant paused?">
    Contact your company administrator. Grants may be paused for administrative reasons.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see my grant history?">
    Yes, all past grants remain visible in your account, including completed grants.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
