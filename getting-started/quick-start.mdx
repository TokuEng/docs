## Admin Quick Start

Get your token grant program running in four steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Log In">
  Access TGA at [tga.app.toku.com](https://tga.app.toku.com) with your admin credentials.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a Vesting Schedule">
  Go to **Schedules** and create your first vesting schedule:
  - Choose a vesting type (cliff, linear, or milestone)
  - Set the vesting period and frequency
  - Save the schedule for reuse

  [Learn more about vesting schedules →](/admin/vesting-schedules)
</Step>

<Step title="Create Your First Grant">
  Go to **Grants** and click **Create Grant**:
  - Enter recipient details
  - Specify token amount
  - Select your vesting schedule
  - Review and create

  [Learn more about creating grants →](/admin/creating-grants)
</Step>

<Step title="Process Distributions">
  When tokens vest, go to **Distributions** to approve and process:
  - Review pending distributions
  - Verify recipient details
  - Approve and execute transfers

  [Learn more about distributions →](/admin/distributions)
</Step>
</Steps>

## Recipient Quick Start

Access your grants and claim tokens in three steps:

<Steps>
<Step title="Log In">
  Access TGA at [tga.app.toku.com](https://tga.app.toku.com) with your credentials.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Up Your Wallet">
  Go to **Settings** and add your wallet address:
  - Enter your wallet address
  - Verify ownership if required
  - Save your settings

  [Learn more about wallet setup →](/recipients/claiming-tokens#setting-up-your-wallet)
</Step>

<Step title="View and Claim">
  From your dashboard:
  - View your grants and vesting progress
  - Claim vested tokens when available
  - Track your claim history

  [Learn more about viewing grants →](/recipients/viewing-grants)
</Step>
</Steps>
