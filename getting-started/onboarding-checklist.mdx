## Getting Started with Toku

Follow this checklist to get your organization set up on Toku.

---

## Initial Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your account">
    You'll receive an invitation email from Toku. Click the link to set up your admin account and create your password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete organization profile">
    Add your company information, including legal name, address, and tax details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add team members">
    Invite other admins who will help manage your Toku account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect integrations">
    Set up your custody provider and any HR system integrations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Product-Specific Setup

### Token Grant Administration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure token details">
    Add your token information including contract address and network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up vesting templates">
    Create standard vesting schedules for your grant programs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect custody">
    Link your custody provider (Fireblocks, Anchorage, etc.) for token distributions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Toku Contractor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure payment methods">
    Set up how you'll pay contractors (bank transfer, crypto, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add contractor templates">
    Create standard contract templates for different engagement types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set approval workflows">
    Define who approves contractor invoices and payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Stablecoin Payroll

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your HR system">
    Link your HRIS to sync employee data automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure payroll settings">
    Set up pay schedules, currencies, and stablecoin options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable employee opt-in">
    Allow employees to choose stablecoin payments.
  </Step>
</Steps>
