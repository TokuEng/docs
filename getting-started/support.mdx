## What to Include

When reaching out, please include:

- **Your email address** associated with your Toku account
- **Organization name** (if you're a client admin)
- **Product area** (TGA, Contractor, or Payroll)
- **Description of the issue** or question
- **Screenshots** if relevant

---

## Response Times

| Priority | Description | Response Time |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Critical** | Can't access account, payments stuck | Within 4 hours |
| **High** | Functionality issues affecting work | Within 1 business day |
| **Normal** | Questions, guidance, feature requests | Within 2 business days |

---

## For Urgent Issues

If you're experiencing a critical issue:

1. Email **support@toku.com** with "URGENT" in the subject line
2. Include your organization name and a brief description
3. Our team will prioritize your request
