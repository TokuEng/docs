## What is Toku?

Toku is the platform for managing token-based compensation and global contractor payments. We help organizations:

- **Issue and manage token grants** with customizable vesting schedules
- **Pay contractors globally** with instant payments in any currency
- **Run stablecoin payroll** with built-in compliance and tax withholding

## Who Uses Toku?

### For Clients (Administrators)
Organization admins use Toku to set up and manage compensation programs, process payments, and ensure compliance.

### For Recipients (Employees, Contractors, Investors)
Recipients use Toku to view their grants, submit invoices, set up wallets, and receive payments.
