## Overview

The TGA employee portal is your hub for viewing token grants, tracking vesting progress, and claiming your tokens.

---

## Getting Started

### First Login

1. Check your email for an invitation from Toku
2. Click the link to set up your account
3. Create a secure password
4. Complete any required identity verification (KYC)

### Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard shows:
- **Total grants** — All grants issued to you
- **Vested tokens** — Tokens you can claim
- **Upcoming vesting** — When more tokens will vest
- **Recent activity** — Claims, distributions, and updates

## Key Actions

### View Your Grants
See all grants you've received, including:
- Token amounts
- Vesting schedules
- Current status

### Track Vesting
Monitor when your tokens vest with a visual timeline and upcoming vesting events.

### Claim Tokens
When tokens vest, submit a claim to receive them in your wallet.

### Manage Wallets
Add and verify wallet addresses where you want to receive tokens.

---

## Notifications

Stay informed with notifications for:
- New grants issued to you
- Vesting events (tokens newly available)
- Claim status updates
- Document availability

Configure notification preferences in **Settings** > **Notifications**.

---

## Security

### Protect Your Account
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Never share your login credentials

### Wallet Security
- Double-check wallet addresses before saving
- Use a secure, self-custody wallet
- Never share your private keys or seed phrase
