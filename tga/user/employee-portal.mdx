## Overview

The TGA employee portal is where you view your token grants, track vesting progress, manage your wallet, and access tax documents.

---

## First Login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    You'll receive an invitation email from Toku with a link to set up your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Click the link, set a secure password, and complete any required identity verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up MFA">
    If your organization requires multi-factor authentication, configure your authenticator app during first login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your grants">
    After login, you'll see your dashboard with all token grants and vesting details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows at a glance:

| Section | What You See |
|---------|-------------|
| **Token Grants** | All grants issued to you with status |
| **Vested Tokens** | Tokens currently available |
| **Upcoming Vesting** | Next vesting dates and amounts |

## Key Actions

### Accept a Grant
When a new grant is issued to you, review the terms and click **Accept**. This starts the vesting clock.

### Set Up Your Wallet
Add your blockchain wallet address so you can receive token distributions. Your wallet must be verified through a test transaction before tokens can be sent.

### Track Vesting
Monitor your vesting progress with a visual timeline showing past and upcoming vesting events.

### Access Documents
View and download grant agreements, settlement confirmations, and tax documents.

---

## Notifications

You'll receive emails for:
- New grants issued to you
- Vesting events (tokens newly available)
- Wallet verification requests
- Settlement confirmations
- Document availability

---

## Security

- **Use a strong password** and enable MFA if available
- **Verify wallet addresses carefully** — tokens sent to wrong addresses cannot be recovered
- **Never share your private keys** or wallet seed phrase with anyone
- **Check the URL** — always ensure you're on the official Toku platform
