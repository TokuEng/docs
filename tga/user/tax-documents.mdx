## Overview

TGA provides tax-related documents to help you report token compensation on your tax returns. Access these documents in the **Documents** section of your portal.

## Accessing Documents

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Documents">
    Click **Documents** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select document type">
    Choose the type of document you need
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select tax year">
    Pick the relevant tax year
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download">
    Click to download or view the document
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Document Details

### Distribution Statements
Shows each token distribution you received:
- Date of distribution
- Number of tokens
- Token value at distribution
- Any withholding applied

### Vesting Reports
Summary of vesting events:
- Vesting dates
- Tokens vested
- Fair market value at vesting

### Exercise Records (Options Only)
For option grants:
- Exercise date
- Number of options exercised
- Strike price paid
- Token value at exercise

---

## Tax Withholding

Your organization may withhold tokens for tax purposes:
- **Withheld amount** shown on distribution statements
- **Net tokens** are what you actually receive
- **Withholding records** available in your documents

---

## Important Dates

| Date | Relevance |
|------|-----------|
| **Vesting date** | When tokens vest — may trigger taxable event |
| **Distribution date** | When you receive tokens |
| **Exercise date** | When you exercise options |
| **Year-end** | Summary documents available in January |

## Tax Considerations

<Warning>
  Token compensation may have significant tax implications. This documentation is for informational purposes only and does not constitute tax advice. Consult a qualified tax professional about your specific situation.
</Warning>

### General Guidance
- Token grants may be taxable at vesting or distribution
- The taxable value is typically the fair market value at the taxable event
- Options may have different tax treatment than RSUs
- Tax rules vary by jurisdiction
