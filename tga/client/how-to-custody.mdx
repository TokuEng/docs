---
title: "How to Connect Custody"
sidebarTitle: "Connect Custody"
description: "Step-by-step walkthrough for setting up a custody integration in TGA"
---

Custody integrations connect TGA to an external wallet or custody provider so tokens can be held, vested, and distributed on-chain. Complete this setup before processing distributions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    In the left sidebar, click **Settings** to expand the settings menu.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/custody/custody-step-1.png" alt="Settings menu expanded in sidebar" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Custody">
    Click **Custody** (or **Integrations**) in the settings submenu. The page shows available custody providers and any active connections.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/custody/custody-step-2.png" alt="Custody providers page showing available integrations" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review available providers">
    TGA supports multiple custody providers. Select the one that matches your organization's custody setup.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/custody/custody-step-3.png" alt="List of supported custody providers" />
    </Frame>

    Supported providers include:
    - **Anchorage Digital**
    - **BitGo**
    - **Coinbase Prime**
    - **Fireblocks**
    - **Self-custody** (direct wallet address)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the connection">
    Click **Connect** next to your provider. Enter the required credentials — typically an API key, wallet address, or OAuth authorization depending on the provider.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/custody/custody-step-4.png" alt="Custody configuration form for a provider" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the connection">
    After saving, TGA verifies connectivity with the custody provider. A green status indicator confirms the connection is active and ready for distributions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Keep your custody API keys secure. Rotate them immediately if they are ever exposed. Update the credentials in TGA Settings → Custody after rotating.
</Warning>
