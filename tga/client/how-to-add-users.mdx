Add recipients to your organization before creating grants. This guide walks through the process from the TGA dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open People Directory">
    In the left sidebar, click **People Directory**. You'll see a list of all users currently in your organization.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/add-users/add-users-step-1.png" alt="People Directory showing the user list" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add User">
    Click the **Add User** (or **Invite User**) button in the top-right of the page.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/add-users/add-users-step-2.png" alt="Add User button highlighted in the People Directory" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the user form">
    Enter the recipient's details in the form:

    - **Email** — Must be unique in your organization
    - **First Name** and **Last Name**
    - **Role** — Select `RECIPIENT` for grant recipients
    - **Country** — Recipient's country of employment
    - **Employment Type** — Full-time, part-time, contractor, etc.
    - **External Employee ID** — Your HRIS identifier (optional, recommended for integrations)

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/add-users/add-users-step-3.png" alt="Add user form with fields filled in" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and confirm">
    Click **Save**. The recipient receives an activation email with a link to set their password and access the employee portal.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/add-users/add-users-step-4.png" alt="Confirmation after user is added successfully" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
To add many users at once, use [Bulk Upload Grants](/tga/client/bulk-upload-grants) — the bulk upload flow also supports uploading recipients from a CSV template.
</Tip>

---

## User Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `RECIPIENT` | Grant recipient — can view grants, manage wallet, see vesting schedule |
| `CLIENT_ORG_ADMIN` | Full access to all admin features |
| `PAYROLL_ADMIN` | Access to payroll and settlement |
| `FINANCE_ADMIN` | Access to reports and payments |
