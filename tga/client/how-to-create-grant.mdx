---

Issue a token grant from the TGA dashboard. You'll need at least one [grant configuration](/tga/client/grant-configurations) and an existing recipient before you start.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Token Grants">
    In the left sidebar, click **Token Grants** to expand the menu, then click **Grants**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/create-grant/create-grant-step-1.png" alt="Token Grants menu expanded in sidebar" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View the grants list">
    The grants list shows all existing grants in your organization — their status, recipient, amount, and vesting progress.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/create-grant/create-grant-step-2.png" alt="Grants list showing existing grants" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click New Grant">
    Click the **New Grant** button in the top-right corner to open the grant creation form.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/create-grant/create-grant-step-3.png" alt="New Grant button highlighted" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a grant configuration">
    Choose the grant configuration that defines the token type and grant structure (RTU, RTA, TPA, or TOKEN_BONUS). If you don't have one yet, see [Setting Up Grant Types](/tga/client/how-to-grant-configurations).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/create-grant/create-grant-step-4.png" alt="Grant configuration selector" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in grant details">
    Complete the grant form:

    - **Recipient** — Search by name or email
    - **Grant Amount** — Number of tokens to award
    - **Grant Name** — A descriptive label (e.g., `Q1 2026 Token Grant - Jane Smith`)
    - **Grant Date** — When the grant was issued
    - **Vesting Start Date** — When vesting begins
    - **Vesting Schedule** — Select a template or enter custom cliff/period settings
    - **Tags** — Optional labels for reporting and filtering

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/tga/create-grant/create-grant-step-5.png" alt="Grant details form filled in" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and create">
    Review the grant summary, then click **Create Grant**. The grant moves to `PENDING` status and the recipient is notified to accept it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
After creation, the grant stays in `PENDING` until the recipient accepts it. You can edit the grant amount, vesting dates, and tags while it's still pending.
</Tip>

---

## Grant Status Reference

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `PENDING` | Awaiting recipient acceptance |
| `ACCEPTED` | Active and vesting |
| `COMPLETED` | Fully vested |
| `TERMINATED` | Ended early by admin |
