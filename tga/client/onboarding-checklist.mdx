## Overview

When you first log in as a Client Admin, the TGA dashboard shows a setup checklist that walks you through everything needed to start managing token grants. Complete all 6 steps to fully configure your organization.

<Frame caption="Dashboard with setup checklist">
  <img src="/images/tga/dashboard/dashboard-overview.png" />
</Frame>

---

## Step 1: Add Employees

Upload your employees who will receive token grants. You can add them one at a time or upload a CSV.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Employees modal">
    Click **Upload** next to "Add Employees" in the checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your method">
    - **Manual entry**: Add employees one by one with name and email
    - **CSV upload**: Upload a spreadsheet with columns for first name, last name, email, and optional fields like department, country, and external employee ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and submit">
    Verify the employee list, fix any validation errors (highlighted in red), then click **Submit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**API:** Use [Create User Role in Org](/api/employees/create-user-role-in-org) to add employees programmatically, or [Batch Update Roles](/api/employees/batch-update-roles) for bulk operations.
</Tip>

---

## Step 2: Create Grant Configuration

Define what type of token grants your organization issues.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a grant type">
    Choose from: **RTU** (Restricted Token Units), **RTA** (Restricted Token Awards), **TPA** (Token Purchase Agreements), or **TOKEN_BONUS**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your configuration">
    A default name is generated from the type. You can customize it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create** to save the configuration. You can create additional configurations later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**API:** Use [List Grant Configurations](/api/grants/list-grant-configurations) to retrieve your configurations.
</Tip>

---

## Step 3: Configure Vesting Schedules

Set up how tokens vest over time using pre-built templates or custom schedules.

**Pre-built templates:**

| Template | Cliff | Vesting | Total Duration |
|----------|-------|---------|----------------|
| 4-Year, 1-Year Cliff | 25% at 12 months | 2.08% monthly | 48 months |
| 3-Year, No Cliff | None | 2.78% monthly | 36 months |
| 2-Year Quarterly | None | 12.5% quarterly | 24 months |
| 1-Year Monthly | None | 8.33% monthly | 12 months |
| Custom | You define | You define | You define |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a template or create custom">
    Select a pre-built template or click **Custom** to open the full vesting builder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the schedule">
    The system generates vesting tranches based on your template. Review the percentage allocations and timing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create** to save the vesting schedule template.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Step 4: Create Grants

Assign token grants to your employees.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Create Grants modal">
    Click **Upload** next to "Create Grants" in the checklist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add grants">
    - **Manual**: Enter email, grant name, and amount for each employee
    - **CSV**: Upload a spreadsheet with grant details including grant type, amount, vesting start date, and optional fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review the grant list and click **Submit** to create all grants.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Grant list after creation">
  <img src="/images/tga/grants/grant-list.png" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
**API:** Use [Add Single Grant](/api/grants/add-single-grant) or [Create Grant for New Hire](/api/grants/create-grant-for-new-hire) to create grants programmatically.
</Tip>

---

## Step 5: Set Up Wallet Settings

Configure which blockchain networks and token contracts your organization uses.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select networks">
    Choose from popular networks: **Ethereum**, **Base**, **Solana**, **Polygon**, **Arbitrum**, **Optimism**, **Avalanche**, or add a custom network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your token address">
    Enter your token's contract address. This is used for distributions and wallet verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply the configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**API:** Use [Get Networks](/api/tokens-and-networks/get-networks) and [Update Token Address](/api/organization/update-token-address) to configure programmatically.
</Tip>

---

## Step 6: Connect Custody Integration

Connect your custody provider for secure token distribution.

**Supported providers:**

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Anchorage" icon="shield-halved">
    Institutional-grade digital asset custody
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fireblocks" icon="fire">
    Enterprise crypto infrastructure
  </Card>
  <Card title="Safe" icon="vault">
    Multisig wallet management
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a provider">
    Select Anchorage, Fireblocks, or Safe from the options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    Follow the inline setup dialog for your chosen provider. You'll need API keys and configuration details from your custody account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and save">
    The system verifies the connection before saving.
  </Step>
</Steps>
