## Welcome to Toku

You've been invited to join Toku by a company that wants to work with you. This guide will help you complete onboarding and get paid.

---

## Completing Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept the invitation">
    Click the link in your invitation email to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Set up your Toku account with your email and a secure password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the contract">
    Read the contractor agreement carefully before signing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign electronically">
    Agree to the terms and sign the contract electronically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add payment information">
    Enter your payment details (bank account, PayPal, or crypto wallet).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    Provide any required identity or tax documentation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Your Dashboard

After onboarding, your dashboard shows:
- **Active engagements** — Companies you're working with
- **Pending invoices** — Invoices awaiting approval
- **Payment history** — Past payments received
- **Documents** — Contracts and tax forms

## Payment Methods

You can receive payments via:
- **Bank Transfer** — Direct deposit to your bank account
- **PayPal** — Instant transfers
- **Crypto** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.
- **Stablecoin** — USDC, USDT

Set up your preferred payment method in **Settings** > **Payment Methods**.

---

## Getting Paid

The typical payment flow:

1. You submit an invoice for work completed
2. Your client reviews and approves
3. Payment is processed via your chosen method
4. You receive confirmation when payment is sent
