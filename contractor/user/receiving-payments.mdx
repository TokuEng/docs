## Overview

Once your invoice is approved, Toku processes your payment according to the schedule and method you've set up.

---

## Payment Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invoice approved">
    Your client approves your submitted invoice
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment scheduled">
    Payment is queued based on the payment schedule
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment processed">
    Toku sends payment via your preferred method
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment received">
    Funds arrive in your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Payment Methods

### Bank Transfer
- Direct deposit to your bank account
- Processing time: 2-5 business days
- Supports most countries and currencies

### PayPal
- Sent to your PayPal email
- Processing time: 1-2 business days
- Standard PayPal fees apply

### Cryptocurrency
- Sent to your crypto wallet address
- Processing time: Same day (network dependent)
- Supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others

### Stablecoin
- Sent to your wallet address
- Processing time: Same day
- Supports USDC, USDT on multiple networks

---

## Setting Up Payment Methods

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Payment Methods">
    Click **Payment Methods**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your method">
    Choose and configure your preferred method:
    - Bank: Enter account and routing numbers
    - PayPal: Enter your PayPal email
    - Crypto: Enter your wallet address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as default">
    Mark your primary payment method as default
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Schedule

Your client sets the payment schedule:
- **On Approval** — Paid immediately when invoice is approved
- **Weekly** — All approved invoices paid weekly
- **Bi-weekly** — Payments every two weeks
- **Monthly** — End-of-month payments

Check your engagement details to see your payment schedule.

---

## Tracking Payments

### Payment Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Invoice approved, payment not yet processed |
| **Processing** | Payment has been initiated |
| **Completed** | Payment sent successfully |
| **Failed** | Payment failed — check your payment method |

### Payment History

View all past payments in **Payments** > **History**:
- Payment date and amount
- Payment method used
- Invoice reference
- Transaction details

---

## Payment Notifications

You'll receive notifications when:
- Your invoice is approved
- Payment is processing
- Payment has been completed
- There's an issue with payment

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment shows 'Failed'">
    Check that your payment details are correct. Update if needed and contact support to retry the payment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Payment is taking longer than expected">
    Bank transfers can take up to 5 business days for international payments. If it's been longer, contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I received less than expected">
    Check for any fees (PayPal fees, bank fees) or withholding that may have been deducted. View payment details for breakdown.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
