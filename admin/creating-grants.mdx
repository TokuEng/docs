## Overview

Token grants allow you to allocate tokens to employees, contractors, or other recipients as part of their compensation package. This guide walks you through creating a new grant in TGA.

## Prerequisites

- Admin access to your organization's TGA account
- Recipient information (name, email, wallet address)
- Grant details (token amount, vesting schedule)

## Creating a New Grant

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Grants">
  From the TGA dashboard, click **Grants** in the sidebar navigation.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Grant">
  Click the **Create Grant** button in the top right corner.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter Recipient Details">
  Fill in the recipient information:
  - **Name**: Recipient's full name
  - **Email**: Recipient's email address
  - **Wallet Address**: The wallet where tokens will be delivered
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Grant Amount">
  Specify the token grant details:
  - **Token Type**: Select the token to grant
  - **Total Amount**: The total number of tokens in the grant
  - **Grant Date**: When the grant is effective
</Step>

<Step title="Select Vesting Schedule">
  Choose a vesting schedule for the grant. See [Vesting Schedules](/admin/vesting-schedules) for details on configuring vesting.
</Step>

<Step title="Review and Create">
  Review all grant details and click **Create Grant** to finalize.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Grant Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Grant created but not yet active |
| **Active** | Grant is active and vesting |
| **Paused** | Grant vesting is temporarily paused |
| **Completed** | All tokens have vested |
| **Cancelled** | Grant has been cancelled |

## Bulk Grant Creation

For creating multiple grants at once:

1. Navigate to **Grants** > **Bulk Import**
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in grant details for each recipient
4. Upload the completed CSV
5. Review and confirm the grants

<Tip>
Use bulk import when onboarding multiple team members or issuing company-wide grants.
</Tip>
