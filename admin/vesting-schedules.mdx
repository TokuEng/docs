## Overview

Vesting schedules determine how and when granted tokens become available to recipients. TGA supports flexible vesting configurations to match your compensation strategy.

## Vesting Schedule Types

### Cliff Vesting

Tokens vest all at once after a specified period.

- **Example**: 1-year cliff = 100% of tokens vest after 12 months
- **Use case**: Ensuring commitment before any tokens are released

### Linear Vesting

Tokens vest gradually over time in equal increments.

- **Example**: 4-year linear monthly = 1/48th of tokens vest each month
- **Use case**: Standard equity compensation schedules

### Milestone-Based Vesting

Tokens vest when specific milestones or conditions are met.

- **Example**: Tokens vest upon product launch, funding rounds, or performance goals
- **Use case**: Tying compensation to company or individual achievements

## Creating a Vesting Schedule

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Schedules">
  From the TGA dashboard, go to **Schedules** in the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step title="Create New Schedule">
  Click **Create Schedule** and select the vesting type.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Parameters">
  Set the schedule parameters:

  **For Cliff Vesting:**
  - Cliff duration (months)
  - Cliff percentage (usually 100%)

  **For Linear Vesting:**
  - Total vesting period
  - Vesting frequency (monthly, quarterly, annually)
  - Optional cliff period

  **For Milestone Vesting:**
  - Define milestones
  - Set percentage for each milestone
</Step>

<Step title="Name and Save">
  Give your schedule a descriptive name and save it for reuse.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Common Vesting Configurations

### Standard 4-Year with 1-Year Cliff

The most common vesting schedule for startups:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Total Period | 4 years |
| Cliff | 1 year (25%) |
| Remaining Vesting | Monthly over 3 years |

### 3-Year Linear Monthly

Faster vesting for contractors or advisors:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Total Period | 3 years |
| Cliff | None |
| Vesting | Monthly (1/36th each month) |

## Managing Existing Schedules

### Viewing Schedules

Navigate to **Schedules** to see all vesting schedules in your organization. Each schedule shows:

- Schedule name and type
- Number of grants using this schedule
- Total tokens under this schedule

### Modifying Schedules

<Warning>
Modifying a schedule affects all grants using that schedule. Consider creating a new schedule instead of modifying existing ones.
</Warning>

To modify a schedule:
1. Click on the schedule name
2. Click **Edit Schedule**
3. Make your changes
4. Review affected grants
5. Confirm changes
