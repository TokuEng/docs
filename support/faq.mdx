## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is TGA?">
    TGA (Token Grant Administration) is a platform for managing token-based compensation. It helps organizations create grants, manage vesting, process payroll, and distribute tokens to employees and contractors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who uses TGA?">
    TGA is used by organizations that pay employees or contractors with tokens, as well as the recipients who receive those tokens.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is TGA secure?">
    Yes. TGA uses institutional-grade custody providers, encryption, and security best practices to protect your tokens and data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Recipients

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I see my grants?">
    Log in to TGA and navigate to the Grants section from your dashboard. You'll see all your active grants, vesting schedules, and claimable tokens.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When can I claim my tokens?">
    You can claim tokens once they've vested according to your grant's vesting schedule. Check your grant details for vesting dates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add a wallet?">
    Go to Wallets in the sidebar, click Add Wallet, and enter your wallet address. You may need to verify ownership.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I entered the wrong wallet address?">
    If you haven't claimed yet, add a new wallet with the correct address. If tokens were already sent to the wrong address, contact support immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is my claim amount less than expected?">
    Your organization may withhold a portion of tokens for tax purposes. Check your claim details for the withholding breakdown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does claiming take?">
    Most claims are processed within 24 hours. Multi-sig wallets may take longer due to approval requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see tokens in my wallet">
    You may need to manually add the token to your wallet using the token contract address. Contact support if you need help.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Admins

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a grant?">
    Navigate to Grants, click New Grant, and follow the wizard to configure the grant type, vesting schedule, and recipient details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I modify a grant after creation?">
    Grants can be modified before the recipient accepts. After acceptance, you'll need to cancel and create a new grant.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I set up payroll?">
    Connect your HRIS in Settings > Integrations, then configure token payroll in the Payroll section.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What custody providers do you support?">
    We support Fireblocks, Anchorage, Coinbase Prime, Paxos, Safe, and Squads. See the [Integrations](/integrations) page for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I process a distribution?">
    Go to Distributions, select the recipients or grants to distribute, review the amounts, and approve for processing.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which blockchains are supported?">
    TGA supports Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, Arbitrum, Optimism, and other major networks. Check with your organization for specifics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are tokens stored?">
    Tokens are held by institutional custody providers (like Fireblocks or Anchorage) until distributed to recipients.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there an API?">
    Contact your Toku account manager for API access and documentation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
