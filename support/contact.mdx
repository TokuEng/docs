## What to Include

When reaching out, please include:

- **Your email address** associated with your TGA account
- **Organization name** (if you're an admin)
- **Description of the issue** or question
- **Screenshots** if relevant

---

## Response Times

| Priority | Response Time |
|----------|---------------|
| Critical (can't access account, tokens stuck) | Within 4 hours |
| High (functionality issues) | Within 1 business day |
| Normal (questions, guidance) | Within 2 business days |

---

## For Urgent Issues

If you're experiencing a critical issue that affects token access or security:

1. Email **support@toku.com** with "URGENT" in the subject line
2. Include your organization name and a brief description
3. Our team will prioritize your request
