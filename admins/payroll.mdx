## Overview

TGA's payroll feature lets you pay employees and contractors in tokens or stablecoins.

---

## Setting Up Payroll

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your HRIS">
    Link your HR system to automatically sync employee data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure payment tokens">
    Select which tokens or stablecoins to use for payroll.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up payroll schedule">
    Define your payroll frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and approve">
    Review payroll before each run and approve for processing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Payment Options

### Token Payroll
Pay employees a portion of their salary in your organization's tokens.

### Stablecoin Payroll
Pay in stablecoins (USDC, USDT) for predictable value.

### Hybrid
Combine fiat, tokens, and stablecoins in a single payroll run.

## Processing Payroll

1. **Review** - Check the payroll summary before processing
2. **Approve** - Admin approval required before distribution
3. **Process** - Tokens are sent to employee wallets
4. **Confirm** - Track distribution status in real-time
