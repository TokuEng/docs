## Overview

Distributions are how tokens get from your organization to recipients. TGA handles the complexity of multi-recipient distributions with built-in compliance.

---

## Distribution Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select recipients">
    Choose which grants or payroll items to distribute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review amounts">
    Verify token amounts and recipient wallet addresses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve distribution">
    Admin approval required before processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    Tokens are sent via your connected custody provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track status">
    Monitor distribution progress in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Distribution Types

### Grant Distributions
Distribute vested tokens from grants to recipients who have claimed them.

### Payroll Distributions
Process token payroll payments to employees and contractors.

### Manual Distributions
One-off distributions for special cases.

---

## Batch Processing

TGA supports batch distributions for efficiency:

- Process multiple recipients in a single transaction
- Reduce gas costs with batching
- Track all distributions in one view

---

## Settlement Methods

Distributions are processed through your connected custody provider:

| Provider | Settlement Time |
|----------|-----------------|
| Fireblocks | Near-instant |
| Anchorage | Same day |
| Coinbase Prime | Same day |
| Safe (Multi-sig) | After approvals |

---

## Tracking Distributions

### Status Types
- **Pending** - Awaiting approval
- **Processing** - Transaction submitted
- **Completed** - Tokens delivered
- **Failed** - Requires attention

### Transaction Details
View blockchain transaction hashes and confirmation status for all distributions.
