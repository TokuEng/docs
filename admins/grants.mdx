## Creating a Grant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Grants">
    From the dashboard, click **Grants** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Grant">
    Select **New Grant** to open the grant creation wizard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select grant type">
    Choose from RTU, Options, Warrants, or other grant types based on your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure vesting">
    Set up your vesting schedule including cliff period and vesting frequency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add recipient">
    Enter the recipient's email address and grant details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and create">
    Review the grant summary and click **Create Grant** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Grant Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **RTU** | Restricted Token Units - tokens that vest over time |
| **Options** | Right to purchase tokens at a set strike price |
| **Warrants** | Similar to options, typically for investors |
| **Token Bonus** | One-time token awards |

---

## Vesting Schedules

TGA supports flexible vesting configurations:

- **Cliff vesting** - Initial period before any tokens vest
- **Linear vesting** - Tokens vest evenly over time (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
- **Milestone vesting** - Tokens vest when specific milestones are achieved

---

## Managing Existing Grants

### View grant details
Click on any grant to see full details including vesting progress and recipient information.

### Modify a grant
Grants can be modified before they're accepted by the recipient. After acceptance, changes require creating a new grant.

### Cancel a grant
Unvested portions of a grant can be cancelled. Vested tokens remain with the recipient.
