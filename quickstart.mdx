## Welcome to TGA

This guide will help you get started based on your role.

---

## For Admins

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to TGA">
    Visit [tga.app.toku.com](https://tga.app.toku.com) and sign in with your organization credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Grants">
    From the dashboard, click **Grants** in the sidebar to view and manage grants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first grant">
    Click **New Grant** and follow the wizard to set up a token grant with vesting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite recipients">
    Add recipients by email. They'll receive an invitation to claim their grant.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Card title="Admin Guide" icon="book" href="/admins/overview">
  Learn more about admin features
</Card>

---

## For Recipients

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    Look for an invitation email from TGA with your grant details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to TGA">
    Click the link in your email or visit [tga.app.toku.com](https://tga.app.toku.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your grants">
    See your grant details, vesting schedule, and claimable tokens on your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Add a wallet address to receive your tokens when you claim them.
  </Step>
</Steps>
